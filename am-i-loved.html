am i loved?

Admin
24 Haziran 2020
0 Comments

am i loved? am i loved?

scattered thoughts and emotions.

i grew up in a household that was distant, emotionally speaking.

‘i love you’ wasn’t a phrase i remember really…

was i even told that as a child?

i most likely was, but it had to have been so rare; it’s completely disappeared from my memories.

i grew up in a household where reassurance wasn’t a thing.

‘i’m proud of you’ or ‘you’re doing good, keep going’ was just too much to ask.

are they even proud of me now? am i at least doing good? should i keep going?

they’ve told me once, and i’ll always remember it. but i was already 18 and it was said, only because i asked them in the first place. yet still, i’ll always savour that warm feeling those words provided me with.

is the way i was brought up the reason i’m desperate for the attention of those around me? and it’s not even that i want to be the center of attention, i actually hate that. my social anxiety can confirm.

it’s more of i want you to know i’m there. i exist. i want to be your friend.

but still, do you want me there? do you like me enough? am i annoying you?

i don’t think i’ll ever get over feeling that way. and maybe the internet isn’t the best way to deal with it either.

but at least i know i can use my voice freely. no restraints, no complaints. my intent is never to harm anyone. on the contrary, take this piece of mine as an example.

i know for a fact i’m not the only one feeling this way. speak up. demand to be heard. but always stay lovely.

you deserved to be loved. but please keep loving.

i may be bitter about my past. however, it’s made me who i am today. and who i am today, is who i am proud of.

i may be rough around the edges, but i’ll keep going. i’ll keep going despite people looking down on me. i’ll keep improving, and definitely failing along the way. but i’m willing to get back up and keep trying.

because trying is all we have.

so keep being you. and embrace all your fails, all your experiences. they’ve made you who you are today. and i assure you, you’re amazing.

not trying to be cheesy or anything. but i can be the reassurance for you. the reassurance that in my childhood and teenage years nobody realized i needed.

yes, i’m proud of you! and you should definitely keep going! no matter how old you are! you are awesome and you shouldn’t give up.

take a break here and there. don’t pressure yourself too much. drink water and don’t forget to eat. sleep enough hours, you deserve to rest.

whatever it is you’re doing in your life when you’re reading this. even if you’re doing nothing at all (cause depression can be that bitch)… know that you’re still valid. your emotions are valid. you are worthy of love and so much more!

please. keep trying. x

just a little reminder for everyone reading and for my future self i guess that life’s an ass but we can get through it.

hope you like this. if you do, please give it a heart or even a reaction :’) i’d really appreaciate it…
