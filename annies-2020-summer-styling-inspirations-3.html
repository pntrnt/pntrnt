let’s get ready for spring and summer!

it’s time to throw away off the shoulder tops and sweaters, and instead bring in all of the new fashion trends! with the warmer weather arriving, it’s time to update our closets. have a read through to find inspirations for all your 2020 fashion desires.

the monochromatic trend is simply wonderful. pair any bottoms with a top of the matching colour to bring your outfit together. to spice it up, add a matching headband as well!

these cardis can be paired with just about anything! they add a touch of elegance to a simple outfit. how gorgeous are these fits!

𝙨𝙡𝙞𝙥 𝙙𝙧𝙚𝙨𝙨𝙚𝙨

a trend where you can make use of elegant and silky slip dresses and tops. slip dresses give off a lovely parisian aesthetic; with the help of a little accessorizing it is perfection.

i adore these small shoulder bags! they work as a perfect addition to an outfit, to give off the nyc street style vibe.
