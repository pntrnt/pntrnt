5 things I learned lately

Admin
24 Haziran 2020
0 Comments

5 things I learned lately 5 things I learned lately

Hi cuties, I’m back!

Today I’m back with an article inspired by @anitta_roxo which i found it encouraging and i really wanted to share what i learned in these last three months.

Since I’ve been in my house for 4 months now due to quarantine i decided that i wanted to build a better version of myself and begin my journey to my goals.

So let’s start!

1. Take time for myself

I always thought that your persona it’s a unique masterpiece in this world and everyone should have that “time” for themeselves. All the time when talking to my friends I remind them to treat no only their bodies but their whole existence, everything about them. However, i always forgot about myself.

I realised that i’ve never spent a day or an hour for my own and i was upset because i was ignoring the first person i should had loved since the beggining. So i really wanted to enjoy those future hours with myself because the past it’s in the past and those lost days sadly i cannot do anything to bring them back.

I began changing my timetable and my whole rutine to try getting used to it. As a result after two months treating myself I feel far more relaxed and loved (from me to me) like being in complete peace. Everything went from doing a better skin rutine to trying to find calm on my mind.

2.To appreciate each moment in your day

I consider myself as an adventurous person who is always up for any activity i’m invited to do. Indeed, where i live it’s considered as one of the most beautiful places of Argentina due to its great variety of fauna and vegetation. Thus maybe that’s why i can’t get tired of discovering new nature trails and amazing waterfalls.

Although i’m in quarantine i’m allow to go outside for a period of time so i try to enjoy everything of my day doing activities related to nature (now). However, this wasn´t like this before, laziness and boredom were completely consuming my energy and willingness of doing activities up to the point i didn’t even get up of bed to at least have breakfast.

This was getting worst but now that i think about it I’ve never realised i was doing literally nothing until one day a friend of mine asked me “what do i do in the day” and well that’s when a guilty feeling and anxiety run through my whole body. Nevertheless, i did change that I struggled but hopefully i could learn how to appreciate each magnificent little detail of my day which made me speechless just because of all the moments i took for granted as something valueless. But believe me that while admiring the mountains covered with bright white snow and silence but only the sound of birds it is something worthy to experience.

3. Compliment my body

About this topic there are a few points i’m still working on but which i thought they were small victories to include and talk about it. Since i remember i was always told to be a certain stereotype that society built and changed through the time.

I grew up thinking that my body wasn’t worthy to love or admire it in front of the mirror. I carried a heavy feeling with me through my whole teen life and at some point i gave up at trying to understand why i couldn’t get rid of this feeling which consumed my confidence, my attitude and self-esteem.

Honestly, it was a tough battle against my own thoughts controlled by this giant ventriloquist called society which was always there to take down everything related to loving myself. It was really frustrating because:

  • Whatever i try on would look horrible when looking at myself in the mirror
  • Avoid going to crowded places
  • Being in a terrible mood all day
  • Annoyed by being asked why i did wear certain clothes or not entering to the swimming pool

Yes, I was suffering inside and i had enough of that so instead of giving up i had a go looking at myself in the mirror and saying “you are more than enough, you are worthy and beautiful in your own way. Look at that incredible skin colour, look at those big thighs. Wow just oustanding”.

Although at first it was hard to finish the sentence i did and i still say it every single morning when i wake up. I compliment those features which make me want to hide but how i already wrote i’m still searching for feeling completely sure of each part of my body.

If you are going through this feeling I want you to know that it’s hard, i know how it feels but please do not give up in searching that powerful feeling when looking at yourself in the mirror and saying ¨Bby you are hot, look at you!”. Have a go with a compliment per day per hour but try it, everything counts. Lastly, be proud of yourself and don’t let anyone define how your body should be, do you.

Each one has its own way to battle this, keep going and never regret choosing your own comfort in your temple.

4. Consistency

I’ve been always a curious kid which fall for everything, when i find something interesting or that really catch my eye i get into it and try to know everything about it. I see it as a positive characteristic of my persona but the dark side of it, it’s that i can’t keep up with whatever is it.

Fortunately, i have friends and my sister who are all the time remininding me my goals, the reason why i do it and encouraging me to continue my journey to my aims. Until now, i could complete and reach some of my goals which motivate me to keep going. Some of them are:

  • Running 10Km four times a week
  • Reach to my 10th day of Chloe Ting challenge
  • A year and a half of being vegetarian
  • Reminding me to focus on my health
  • Finishing to write 10 songs of mine (i’m utterly proud of this one)
  • Read 2 books in a month (a small victory 🙂 )

Well, and so on, but my main point is to encourage you to go for those goals that you maybe have set up. Do not compare yourself with others, do you and you’ll see wins.

5. To accept

As a human we have ups and downs in life, and it’s ok. Things happen for a reason and there is always a solution if you are brave and able to see the situation with another perspective. We are not born knowing how to solve or better said face tough moments that life want us to confront, and it’s ok.

This last thing that i learnt i believe it’s one of the most complex features in my life that i had to understand. The reason why it’s that I’ve never been strict with my decisions and that allowed people control my life. It’s sad to think about it but i know i should accept how some situations went, accept things i did wrong and also accept people’s reactions and actions. Instead of searching a guilty or an innocent, i search for a reason why things happen like that and just accept it because i can’t do anything about the past but accept that i’m the only one who can change the future to not go through the same situation as well.

To accept things can be a difficult step but it’s not impossible, at some point we all have hardship situations and that’s ok. Once you accept where you are, you’ll be able to understand future scenarios.

Thanks for reading, see you next time!
