A leaf on the wind A leaf on the wind

I’ve always felt like I’m not in control of my life.

Never truly held my destiny with my own hands.

It always felt like I was thrown into this world by chance,

So what happens, happens.

I’m still struggling with the sense of purpose.

Struggling to find my place in this world.

I found one in my imagination, but you can’t always escape reality.

It appears in front of your eyes so often,

And you can’t keep your eyes shut all the time.

Sometimes you have to be vulnerable.

You need to stay soft.

It makes you beautiful.

But you still feel like you are out of control.

Just another little leaf on the wind…

– Mikaela
