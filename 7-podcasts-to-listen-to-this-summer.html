7 Podcasts To Listen To This Summer

Admin
24 Haziran 2020
0 Comments

7 Podcasts To Listen To This Summer 7 Podcasts To Listen To This Summer

Hi everybody,

I hope you’ve been doing okay! I love listening to podcasts whenever I’m too tired to read, or during bus rides back from school, or even just while doing the dishes. Podcasts are a great way to learn more about your favorite topic, see things in a new light, or just broaden your view on the world. Today I’ll be talking about some podcasts to listen to this summer, or any time really, that I highly recommend! I’ve made sure that there are different styles of podcasts and different kinds of topics so that there might be something in there for everyone! I hope you’ll find something you like! And as always; stay safe!

NB. You can listen to all of these podcasts on Spotify and are all in English.

Pod Save the People with DeRay

Host(s): DeRay McKesson

Genre: society, culture

In this podcast activist, DeRay McKesson talks about social justice, culture, and politics and explains the steps each of us can take to make a difference. He reports on issues that aren’t highlighted very much in traditional news outlets and gives the listener a thoughtful analysis of the topic. Apart from his usual team, some notable guests have been John Legend and Edward Snowden.

The History Chicks

Host(s): Beckett Graham & Susan Vollenweider

Genre: history, documentary

Two amazing women, Beckett Graham and Susan Vollenweider, teaching you about some more amazing women in a pretty lighthearted way. Enough about all the battles men have won, let’s talk about the women throughout history. This podcast sparks an interest to research more about these women, ranging from Cleopatra to Cinderella, from Lizzie Borden to Audrey Hepburn and every woman in between.

Conspiracy Theories

Host(s): Molly Brandenburg & Carter Roy

Genre: history, true crime

The first line in their description somehow immediately pulled me in; “The truth is rarely the best story. And when it’s not the only story, the truth deserves another look”. This is one of my personal favorites. The show is about well-known, controversial events that happened in the world and questions if there was more going on. From Princess Diana to Area 51, you should stay focused if you want to understand everything that’s going on, but surely this podcast will intrigue you and focusing won’t be difficult at all.

Let’s Talk About Myths, Baby! A Greek & Romand Mythology Podcast

Host(s): Liv

Genre: history, comedy

In this podcast, the hilarious host Liv tells the stories of Greek and Roman mythology and places them in a contemporary light. She talks about the crazy things the gods did (and got away with, what?!), the heroes and the poor women having to put up with the horny gods and best of all, she tells all of this with the perfect amount of sarcasm! Absolutely amazing podcast if you want to properly learn more about Greek and Roman mythology, but you want to keep it light (because trust me, it can get heavy if you don’t watch out!).

Zero To Travel Podcast

Host(s): Jason Moore

Genre: society, travel, culture

Travel is a luxury a lot of people can’t live without anymore. But many have lost their way going from place to place. In this podcast, Jason Moore discusses what travel actually is, why we do it, and how to do it better. Sure we love to dream away about far off destinations, but it’s important to stand still and think about how our travels are affecting the environments and cultures we come in touch with. It’s important that we consider how to travel more consciously and sustainably, and listening to this podcast is a great start!

George Ezra & Friends

Host(s): George Ezra

Genre: society, culture

Sometimes I’m in that mood where I feel down or empty and drained and don’t feel like listening to anything else, I just want to listen to a podcast with people that I know and look up to. This podcast is my go-to if I’m in that kind of mood. It’s light and I won’t need to concentrate 100% to understand what is happening. In this podcast, one of my favorite musicians, George Ezra, goes in conversation with a fellow musician and talks about, how could it be anything else, music of course. Even though you don’t know these people personally, you immediately get the feeling that you’re listening to a genuine conversation between two people, and that you’re in some ways, part of it. One minute they’re joking around and laughing, next minute they’re talking about the deep meaning of lyrics or their songwriting process. Some of my favorite guests have been Niall Horan, Lewis Capaldi, Ed Sheeran, Elton John and more. Certainly worth a listen!

Harry Potter and the Sacred Text

Host(s): Vanessa Zoltan & Casper ter Kuile

A very intriguing podcast that I highly recommend to any fan of Harry Potter. This podcast gives a bookclub kind of feel, but now it’s time to look at Harry Potter in a different light. In every episode, a new chapter of the book we’ve probably read a hundred times gets discussed. They are always centered around a theme. They’ll talk about things such as commitment, revenge, forgiveness, and more and tries to use these chapters as inspirational texts trying to teach us about our own lives. The podcast description ends on a pretty inspirational quote by itself: “Because reading fiction doesn’t just help us escape the world, it helps us live in it.”.

I hope you’ve found something you will like, obviously there are many, many more great podcasts out there to listen to, and I’d love to recommend more. See you soon and keep safe!
