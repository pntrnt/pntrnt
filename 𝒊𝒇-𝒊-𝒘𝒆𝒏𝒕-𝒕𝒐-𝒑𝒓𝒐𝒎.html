Well, I guess it’s too bad. A lot of us were never able to get a prom and it fucking sucks, but it’s just something we have to deal with. This isn’t actually what it would be like if I did go to prom. It would have been way less cool and extravagant.

promposal?

If you have been reading my articles then you know that the only “love interest” in my life doesn’t like me back so no promposal for me. But I always liked the idea of him jokingly singing Can’t Take My Eyes Off You by Frankie Valli on a football field like on 10 Things I Hate About You.

dress?

If I had the guts, I think I would wear a silk dress with a cowl neckline.

shoes?

Silver, black, or silver and black heels. I don’t really know how to walk in them but a girl can dream.

hair?

Half up half down is like my go-to hairstyle for everything. I guess with prom I can curl my hair with it (even though my hair is naturally wavy and poofy)? I kinda want to look somewhat natural.

accessories?

dainty necklace, bulky rings, my charm bracelet (all silver).

nails?

black glittery acrylic

makeup?

date?

songs i wanted to hear?

outwest – jackboys & travis scott

crank that (soulja boy) – soulja boy tell ’em

my type – saweetie

baby – quality control, lil baby, & dababy

mr. brightside – the killers

look at me – xxxtentacion

afterparty

probably a couple of parties, go get whataburger, and then a sleepover after.
