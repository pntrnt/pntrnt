]]> 18 things i learned in 18 years https://pinternet.org/18-things-i-learned-in-18-years.html Wed, 24 Jun 2020 06:18:43 +0000 https://pinternet.org/18-things-i-learned-in-18-years/ 18 things i learned in 18 years 18 things i learned in 18 years

Hi loves! If you read the title and were curious to know what an 18 year old girl could possibly have learned in 18 years, I’m going to answer that for you right now by saying a whole lot.

I was doing my self-care routine and listening to the new chloe x halle album (cause they’re so talented omg we stan) and I realized I’m turning 19 in a few weeks. At first, the thought was scary and I almost dropped my iced matcha latte (thank goodness I didn’t) but then I realized I’m grateful for how these 18 years have gone. Of course, there have been ups and downs but I’ve really learned a lot and grown as a person and hope to continue to do so in my 19th year. So anyway here it is, my list of 18 things:

1. Life goes on, no matter what you’re feeling inside: It may seem like the world stops as soon as something terrible happens to you but I promise it doesn’t

2. Failure is good: It’s better to try and fail a million times than to be too scared to ever put yourself out there

3. Not everyone is who they seem to be: There’s a lot of good people in the world of course, but I’ve learned that there are equal amounts of bad people

4. People make mistakes: Yes, someone may have done something to hurt you but it isn’t always intentional

5. Keep your head up: No matter what you’re going through, try to keep a brave face because it can even motivate you in your worst times

6. Be kind: Life’s too short to be bitter babe

7. Notice your strengths as well as your weaknesses: It may be easy to point out your flaws only but in the long run, you’ll do better highlighting your strengths as well

8. Some people aren’t meant to be in your life forever: and that’s okay

9. Take care of your body: When you take care of it, I promise it takes care of you

10. Laugh more: You may hate your laugh but the world would be lost without it

11. Tell your friends you love them: You’d be surprised who’s life you’re saving

12. Learn to be alone: Spend time with yourself and get to know yourself and you’ll be surprised at what you find

13. Stop comparing yourself to others: You’ll never be them and they’ll never be you and that’s how it’s supposed to be

14. Take mental health breaks: It’s okay to not be okay sometimes

15. Read more: There’s a whole world in books that you don’t find anywhere else

16. Work harder: There’s always going to be someone who wants it more than you

17. Smile at yourself in the mirror every day: It may sound stupid but you’d be surprised at the effect it has on your brain

And finally,

18. Love yourself: Because no one is going to do it for you

]]> summer: expectations vs. reality https://pinternet.org/summer-expectations-vs-reality.html Wed, 24 Jun 2020 04:44:51 +0000 https://pinternet.org/summer-expectations-vs-reality/ summer: expectations vs. reality summer: expectations vs. reality

Summer vacation is simple to describe. It’s that 2-3 month break students get from school, life, and pretty much anything else they desire to escape from. Okay, not literally, but it’s still a break that we get from the huge amounts of homework, classwork, and coursework that get forced on us in those months of required schooling. It’s highly understandable why people freak out when it’s time for summer vacation because, really, everyone loves a break. However, there is a huge gap between what most people think their summer will be like versus how it actually turns out.

I honestly blame High School Musical 2. I was nine years old when that movie came out and I’d never really thought about my summer in clear detail until my nine-year-old eyes glanced upon the dancing and singing bodies of the High School Musical cast. There they were yelling “summer” over again, while banging their hands on the desks and getting ready to sing and dance. I know for a fact that I would have gotten expelled if I had so much as tried the things they did in that movie. Though, I guess that’s where the debate comes into play.

So here are a few expectations vs. reality comparisons about summer vacation:

Expectation: Going to tons of summer parties with your friends

Reality: Going to three or four parties in the beginning and then spending quality time with the internet the rest of the summer

Expectation: Having a summer romance

Reality: Watching romance movies all summer to try and forget the non-existence of your love life or having a two-month fling with a nicotine-addicted frat boy

Expectation: Finally reading the books on your wish list

Reality: Netflix, Hulu, YouTube, and pretty much every other streaming site available

Expectation: Doing cool, aesthetic things with your friends

Reality: Sleeping for 10 hours, waking up, then sleeping again

Expectation: Having the perfect summer body

Reality: There is no perfect summer body, your body as it is right now is perfect ♡

That was an example of one of the many things that happen to human beings as we’re just trying to get through our everyday life. Sometimes life is good to us. However, other times life is somewhat a struggle and can be quite hard to get through without a little help here and there. This is where I come in to give some amazing life hacks that could potentially save you one day, or could at least save you the hassle of doing things the harder way.

Life Hacks:

1. Tie your scarves to hangers for easy storage during Spring and Summer, so that during the colder months you won’t have to look for them.

2. Use socks and a hairdryer to break in and loosen up your new flats in just a few minutes.

3. Research shows that eating a kiwi fruit before bed has sleep and health benefits.

4. Several studies showed that smelling an orange can reduce stress and improve your mood.

5. Tie your hair loosely to avoid bald spots.

6. Don’t stare at your phone before bed. The reasoning behind it is that the lighting that is used to power phones is relative to the lighting projected in our minds during day time, so staring at our phones makes our brain think it isn’t time to go to bed. But yeah social media is not do or die, wait until morning guys.

7. Baking soda absorbs the stink in your shoes and can get rid of any dust and debris that could accumulate while your shoes are in storage.

8. Running your mascara under hot water for 30 seconds (closed) can un-clump it and refresh and old applicator brush.

9. Sleeping in a cold room can boost your metabolism and amplify fat burning. Also taking cold showers.

10. Finally, drink a glass of hot water with lemon as soon as you wake up because it’ll detoxify your system, leave you feeling fresh, boost your immune system, and give you an all-around great start to your day.
