Travel in time

I remember I used to tell my mom that I wanted to stop to time, to relive all those happy moments, to never grow up like peter pan but then she told me that the only way to feel that you can stop time is to enjoy every moment, to be kind and believe that there is someone out there looking for us, at that time I didn’t care that much I only wanted to keep playing.

Find the monster under my bed

When I was little I believed that there was something under my bed for me my monster was a wich who would take me away, I used to sleep with a night light but as the time pass I grow out of the fear to know I look back in time and think that maybe we all believe there’s a monster under our bed to makes us enjoy every single second of the daytime and make most of it joyful.

Marry a prince

I accept it I was one of the little girls who wanted to get married to a prince and be a princess with her happily ever after know that I think about it I do see myself getting married in a point of my life but before that, I want to achieve all my dreams, be able to success by myself and accomplish all my goals.

]]> Day 2: People who inspire me https://pinternet.org/day-2-people-who-inspire-me.html Wed, 24 Jun 2020 05:22:42 +0000 https://pinternet.org/day-2-people-who-inspire-me/ Day 2: People who inspire me Day 2: People who inspire me

Ello everyone,

Today’s article is day two of my 30-day writing challenge.

1. My partner

As cliched as it could sound, he is one of my biggest inspirations. He fights and works hard for his dreams even when people may discourage it or not fully believe it. He is my biggest supporter and teaches me so many valuable lessons but he also encourages me to come out of my comfort zone and experience things I am terrified of. He is one of the strongest and kindest humans I have ever met and everyday he wakes up and tries to be the best human he can be and I am so blessed that I get to be beside him everyday cheering him on.

2. Strong women

I am very grateful that growing up I had a strong woman in my life to look up too and aspire to be one day, and for that I thank you Mam. My mother has survived many difficulties but she never let them define her, she stands up for herself when she is being treated less than she should, she is courageous and has raised three insanely stubborn and strong women. I hope that I can continue to grow and help other women realise how strong they are and that no ones words or actions define them.

3. Anyone who decides to be their true self

Showing yourself to the world is scary and anyone who does that inspires me because it takes strength. They know themselves and they’ve accepted themselves and want to show their amazing self to the world. Not everyone will like everyone and that’s okay but simply deciding to show yourself and not hide inspires me. It is people like that who have inspired me too slowly show my true self.
