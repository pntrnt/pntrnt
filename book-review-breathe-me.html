Hi everyone, I’m becky and this will be my first book review I post on WHI.

So you know my favourite book genre are Romance/Dark romance, Thriller/Mystery, Ya Contemporary, I also love books that are about LGBTQ and Mental Health etc. I also love books with high Teen Drama/Friendship.

I also want to get into fantasy and already have a few books in mind.

I have a Bookstagram account @abookhuntress please go check it out. I already have a few reviews and book edits on there. Thank you and I appreciate you taking the time to read this article

Now let’s begin

Book Name – Breathe me

Authors -Ivy Fox & C R Jane [2 authors wrote this book]

This book is a Romance/Reverse Harem story

A romance between a girl and three other guys.

If you don’t like love triangles etc don’t worry you still might enjoy this because it’s not a book where they all dare each other. It’s the opposite.

This book is also a duet and the 2nd book will be released on July 30th.

You can buy this book on amazon/Kindle

This book actually was just released, I was given an arc for this book. Make sure you check it out

BOOK DESCRIPTION

They say that true love only comes once in a life time. In my case, they were wrong. I gave my heart away three times. To the blue eyed sensitive soul, who read poetry when he thought no one was looking. To the green eyed jock, who made you laugh as hard as he made you cry. And to the dark eyed boy, whose dark heart only ever beat for me. They were my everything. My bestfriends my soulmates until they weren’t. They asked me to choose but they were asking the impossible. Now it’s my turn to ask for more than what they were willing to give. I only have three months left and I want to spend it with them. Will they accept my offer or will four hearts live the remaining days of their lives in regret of what could have been. The letters have been sent. All that remains is for me to wait. Wait to see if there hearts are still mine. Because for as long as mine beats, it will always belong to them.

REVIEW

I loved this book. It was very engaging. Very well written especially with the pathing between past and present. Normally with a book that has multiple men/love interests I can always pick a favourite, one that I’m routing for or just one I connect with the most. But no not with this book. Each boy will capture your hearts and never let go. It was an emotional ride especially with everything each character has been through with family and themselves. Each had a beautiful and heart breaking story.

I don’t want to spoil anything of course but trust me each character is different and very compelling. Logan is Sweet/Caring Carter is Dark/Intense and Quaid is Hilarious/Playful.
