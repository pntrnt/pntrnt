BE A PART OF WHI ANONYMOUS BE A PART OF WHI ANONYMOUS

I’m so happy to see that some of you have taken the initiative, to be a part of this community. But I’d love a lot more participation from other hearters, writers and readers here.

If you have anything you’re facing or need help on, you can confess through this google form. Be it a problem you’re facing or something you couldn’t share with anyone else. Everything will be anonymous, so let your heart out:

https://forms.gle/aiRU9279jMH6jL6R8

I have created this community with the thought, of having every person on this website be a part of it, one way or another.

Doesn’t matter if you’re a Hearter, a Reader or a Writer, there’s a role for each one of you.

And through this article, I’d be telling you what your role could be and how you can participate in this initiative.

As a Hearter

As a hearter, you’ll be sharing the words of our writers, you can heart and promote these article, to all of your followers who might’ve missed them.

The Articles in this community are made by the heaters on this website. The people who share things about themselves that they couldn’t with anyone else.

You can spread these words, thoughts and problems so that if someone is out there, having faced something similar can reach out and help.

You can also be a part of the We heart it anonymous team, To apply fill out this google form, last date to apply is 25th June,

https://forms.gle/QjhpdWED8QifkTkr6

And if you want to share anything anonymously, you can do it here;

https://forms.gle/aiRU9279jMH6jL6R8

As a Reader

As a reader, you have the biggest role. You’re the one whose reading these articles and understanding these wonderful hearters, who’re facing something.

How can you help?

I have introduced a comment section as part of the articles, it will be a google form where you can write your reply, motivating and helping the writers.

Your comments will be displayed on the article itself, for the writer to read and be motivated.

You can make these comments either anonymously, or you give your name with the comments if you want.

This way you can help the writer and give them the support and motivation, they deserve.

You can also be a part of the We heart it anonymous team, To apply fill out this google form, last date to apply is 25th June,

https://forms.gle/QjhpdWED8QifkTkr6

And if you want to share anything anonymously, you can do it here;

https://forms.gle/aiRU9279jMH6jL6R8

As a Writer

As a writer you are always welcome to help make WHI anonymous a better place.

But you can also participate through, giving inputs in the comment section and helping out the people in this community, through the comment section.

You can also help us spread the word about this community, so that if anyone wants to share something, they can reach out to us.

If you want to be a part of the We heart it anonymous team, To apply fill out this google form, last date to apply is 25th June,

https://forms.gle/QjhpdWED8QifkTkr6

And if you want to share anything anonymously, you can do it here;

https://forms.gle/aiRU9279jMH6jL6R8

Thank you so much for reading this article.

I’d love to have more heaters, taking part.

If you have a doubt you can always reach out,

https://weheartit.com/messages/aeonian_desires
